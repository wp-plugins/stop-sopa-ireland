This plugin adds a banner to your site letting people know your support of the stop SOPA Ireland campaign

== Description ==

Even though SOPA in the US has been shelved (for now) a similar law is being sneaked into Irish law without any discussion. This plugin lets you show your opposition to it.

== Installation ==

1. Unzip stop-sopa-ireland.zip to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
